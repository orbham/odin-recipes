Chocolate Chip Cookies

Introduction:

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever! buttery, slightly doughy, & so good. So easy with no weird ingredients or chilling.

Ingredients You'll Need:

  • Melted butter
  • Egg and egg yolk
  • Brown and white sugars
  • Chocolate chips
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Flour
  • Vanilla
  • Cornstarch

Baking Steps:

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown

How Long to Bake?

With the oven preheated at 350 degrees, we recommend to bake for 10 minutes.

Tips:

Refrigerate your dough at least overnight. This gives the flavors a chance to develop!
