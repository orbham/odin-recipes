Pecan Pie

Introduction

Pecan pie is a classic American dessert that features pecans baked in a buttery, flaky crust with a filling of eggs, butter, and sugar. It's the perfect blend of sweet and delicious!

Ingredients You'll Need:

  • One cup packed brown sugar
  • A third of a cup of melted butter
  • Three fourths of a cup of light corn syrup
  • One half teaspoon of salt
  • Three eggs
  • One and one half cups of pecans
  • Pie crust

Baking Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 375Â°F; place cookie sheet in oven to preheat
  2. In medium bowl, beat brown sugar, butter, corn syrup, salt and eggs with whisk until well blended; stir in pecans
  3. Pour into frozen crust
  4. Bake on preheated cookie sheet 35 to 45 minutes or until center is set
  5. After 15 to 20 minutes of baking, cover crust edge with strips of foil to prevent excessive browning
  6. Cool 30 minutes; refrigerate at least 2 hours until chilled before serving
  7. Cover and refrigerate
