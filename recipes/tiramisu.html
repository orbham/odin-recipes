Tiramisu

Description:

Tiramisu is an elegant and rich layered Italian dessert made with delicate ladyfinger cookies, espresso or instant espresso, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, Marsala wine, rum and cocoa powder. Tasting coffee-like, it's a treat everyone can enjoy!

Ingredients You'll Need:

  • Ladyfinger's
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy-Whipping Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
